## Why Shallotte Homes Need Gutter Guards

Shallotte's coastal location and mature tree canopy create the perfect storm for gutter problems:

- **Pine needles** — they slip through standard gutter covers and pack tight
- **Oak and maple leaves** — heavy loads that clog downspouts
- **Coastal debris** — palm fronds, twigs, and seed pods
- **Heavy rainfall** — 50+ inches/year means clogs cause overflow fast

Without gutter guards, you're cleaning gutters 3-4 times per year. With professional gutter protection, most Shallotte homeowners clean once a year or less.

## Gutter Guard Options We Install in Shallotte

### Micro-Mesh Gutter Guards (Best for Pine Needles)
Our most popular option for Shallotte homes. Fine stainless steel mesh blocks even pine needles while allowing maximum water flow.

- Blocks debris down to 50 microns
- Self-cleaning design - debris blows off the surface
- Works with all gutter types
- **Lifetime warranty** on materials

### Reverse Curve (Surface Tension) Guards
Water flows over a curved hood into the gutter while debris slides off.

- No moving parts
- Handles heavy leaf loads well
- Best for homes under heavy tree canopy

### Foam Gutter Guards
Economical solution that fits inside existing gutters.

- Easy installation on existing gutters
- Blocks leaves and large debris
- Budget-friendly option
