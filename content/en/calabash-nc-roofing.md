## Coastal Roofing Considerations for Calabash

Calabash sits between the Intracoastal Waterway and the Atlantic Ocean — close enough for salt air to affect roofing materials. Here's what we recommend:

- **Fasteners:** Marine-grade stainless steel or hot-dipped galvanized (not standard galvanized)
- **Flashing:** Aluminum or Kynar-coated steel (not standard galvanized)
- **Shingles:** Architectural with algae-resistant coating (copper granules)
- **Underlayment:** Synthetic with self-adhering membrane at eaves and valleys
- **Fastening:** 6-nail application minimum for wind resistance
