# Roof Financing Options in North Carolina

A new roof is one of the most important investments you can make in your home — but we understand that the upfront cost can be a concern. That's why **Mendoza Roofing LLC** offers multiple financing options to make quality roofing affordable for every homeowner in North Carolina.

Whether you're dealing with storm damage, planning a proactive replacement, or upgrading to a FORTIFIED Roof, we'll help you find a payment solution that fits your budget.

## Financing Options Available

### Monthly Payment Plans
Spread the cost of your new roof over manageable monthly payments:
- **Flexible terms:** 12, 24, 36, or 60-month options
- **Competitive rates:** Low-interest financing for qualified homeowners
- **Quick approval:** Many approvals in minutes
- **No prepayment penalties:** Pay off early and save on interest

### Deferred Payment Options
Some financing programs offer deferred payment periods:
- **No payments for 6-12 months** on qualifying projects
- **0% promotional periods** available
- Ideal for homeowners who need time to arrange funds

### Home Equity Options
If you have equity in your home:
- **HELOC (Home Equity Line of Credit):** Flexible borrowing against home equity
- **Home equity loan:** Lump-sum financing at competitive rates
- **Potential tax advantages:** Interest may be tax-deductible (consult your tax advisor)

## Insurance Claim Assistance

Many roof replacements in North Carolina are covered by homeowners insurance. We make the process easier:

### Free Storm Damage Inspection
After any significant weather event, we offer **free roof inspections** to assess damage:
- Thorough documentation of all damage
- Photo evidence for your insurance claim
- Honest assessment of what's covered vs. wear-and-tear

### Insurance Claim Support
We work directly with you and your insurance company:
- **Meet with your adjuster** on-site to ensure all damage is documented
- **Provide detailed estimates** that meet insurance company requirements
- **Advocate for you** throughout the claims process
- **Handle supplements** if additional damage is discovered during work

### When Insurance Covers Your Roof
Your homeowners insurance typically covers roof damage caused by:
- Wind storms and hurricanes
- Hail damage
- Falling objects (trees, branches)
- Fire and lightning
- Weight of ice, snow, or sleet

**Note:** Normal wear and tear, age-related deterioration, and lack of maintenance are typically not covered.

## NC FORTIFIED Grant Assistance

The **NC FORTIFIED grant program** helps coastal homeowners offset the cost of upgrading to a FORTIFIED Roof:

### What's Available
- **Up to $6,000** in grant funding in some coastal counties
- **Applies to FORTIFIED Roof upgrades** (enhanced deck attachment, sealed roof deck, reinforced connections)
- **Available to homeowners** in designated coastal counties

### How We Help
Mendoza Roofing guides you through the entire process:
1. **Eligibility assessment** — determine if your home and county qualify
2. **Application assistance** — we help complete and submit your application
3. **Inspection coordination** — schedule required pre- and post-installation inspections
4. **Installation** — we build your FORTIFIED Roof to IBHS standards
5. **Certification** — you receive your FORTIFIED certificate for insurance discounts

### Insurance Discounts on Top of Grants
In addition to grant funding, FORTIFIED Roof certification typically qualifies you for:
- **15-30% reduction** in homeowners insurance premiums
- **Long-term savings** that can offset the initial investment
- **Increased home value** and marketability

## Roof Replacement Cost Guide (NC 2026)

Understanding typical costs helps you plan:

### Asphalt Shingle Roof
- **3-tab shingles:** $5,000-$10,000 (20-25 year lifespan)
- **Architectural shingles:** $8,000-$18,000 (25-30 year lifespan)
- **Impact-resistant shingles:** $10,000-$22,000 (30-40 year lifespan)

### Metal Roofing
- **Standing seam steel:** $15,000-$35,000 (40-70 year lifespan)
- **Aluminum standing seam:** $18,000-$40,000 (50+ year lifespan)
- **Metal shingles:** $12,000-$28,000 (40-60 year lifespan)

### Other Materials
- **TPO/EPDM (flat roofs):** $8,000-$20,000 (20-30 year lifespan)
- **Tile (clay/concrete):** $20,000-$50,000+ (50+ year lifespan)
- **Wood shake:** $15,000-$30,000 (25-40 year lifespan)

**Note:** These are rough estimates. Actual costs vary based on roof size, pitch, complexity, decking condition, and material choices. We provide free, detailed estimates with no obligation.
