# Roofing in Southport, NC

Southport is one of the most charming communities in North Carolina — and one of the most exposed to coastal weather. The combination of salt air from the Cape Fear River, intense summer sun, and hurricane-force winds requires a roofing contractor who truly understands the local environment.

## Roofing Services in Southport NC

### Residential Roofing
We handle all residential roofing in Southport, from simple repairs to full replacements. Our work covers Southport's historic downtown homes, waterfront properties, and newer construction in areas like St. James Plantation.

### Storm Damage & Hurricane Repair
Southport sits at the mouth of the Cape Fear River, making it particularly vulnerable to direct hurricane landfalls. Our 24/7 emergency storm response team is ready to tarp and stabilize your roof within hours of a storm passing.

### Metal Roofing for Coastal Homes
We strongly recommend **aluminum standing seam or corrugated metal** for homes within 2 miles of the water. Metal is salt-air resistant and handles high-wind uplift far better than asphalt shingles.

### Commercial Roofing
For businesses along Howe Street and industrial properties near the port, we offer flat roof TPO systems, maintenance contracts, and detailed inspection reports for insurance purposes.
