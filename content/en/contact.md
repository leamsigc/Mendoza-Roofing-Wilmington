## Contact & Estimate FAQ

### What information should I include when contacting Mendoza Roofing?

Please tell us your property address, roof type if you know it (asphalt shingles, metal, flat/TPO, etc.), and whether you are seeing active leaks, missing shingles, or storm damage. The more detail you share, the easier it is for us to prepare for your inspection and provide an accurate, line-item estimate.

### How quickly will someone follow up?

During normal business hours (Monday-Friday, 7am-6pm), our team usually responds to online requests the same day. For after-hours emergencies such as active leaks or storm damage, call [910-367-7628](tel:9103677628) directly. We provide urgent support across Wilmington and Brunswick County.

### Does Mendoza Roofing charge for roof estimates?

No. Roof inspections and written estimates are **completely free** for homeowners and businesses in Wilmington, Supply, Shallotte, Leland, Oak Island, Southport, and the surrounding Brunswick County communities. There is no obligation after an inspection.

### What types of roofing work do you handle?

We handle the full range of residential and commercial roofing work, including:
- Roof repair for leaks, storm damage, and missing shingles
- Full roof replacement (asphalt shingles, metal, tile, TPO/flat)
- New construction roofing
- Emergency roof repair after hurricanes and severe storms
- Gutter installation and replacement
- Commercial flat roof systems (TPO, EPDM, Modified Bitumen)

### What areas does Mendoza Roofing serve?

Mendoza Roofing serves all of **Brunswick County, NC** — including Wilmington, Supply, Shallotte, Leland, Oak Island, Southport, and neighboring coastal communities. We have been operating out of Supply, NC since 1974.
