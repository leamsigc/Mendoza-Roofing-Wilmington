## Holden Beach Roofing Challenges

### Direct Atlantic Exposure
Holden Beach faces the open Atlantic with no offshore islands or shoals to buffer storms. This means:
- **Maximum wind exposure** — storms hit with full force
- **Salt spray** — accelerates corrosion of all metal components
- **Driving rain** — water intrusion risk is higher than inland locations
- **UV intensity** — unobstructed sun exposure degrades materials faster

### Hurricane & Tropical Storm Risk
Holden Beach has been impacted by numerous named storms. Your roof must withstand:
- Sustained winds of 74-130+ mph
- Wind gusts exceeding 150 mph in major hurricanes
- Torrential rainfall (5-15+ inches in a single storm)
- Storm surge effects on lower-elevation structures

### Strict Building Codes
Holden Beach enforces some of the strictest building codes in North Carolina:
- Higher wind speed design requirements (140+ mph)
- Enhanced fastening requirements
- Corrosion-resistant material mandates
- Permit and inspection requirements for all roofing work

## FORTIFIED Roof on Holden Beach

The IBHS FORTIFIED Roof program is ideal for Holden Beach barrier island homes:

1. **Enhanced roof deck attachment** — ring-shank nails at 4-6 inch spacing
2. **Sealed roof deck** — self-adhering membrane at eaves, valleys, and penetrations
3. **Reinforced roof-to-wall connections** — enhanced clips and connectors

**Holden Beach FORTIFIED benefits:**
- Withstands 140+ mph winds (covers every storm in NC history)
- 15-30% insurance premium discounts
- NC FORTIFIED grants up to $6,000 in some coastal counties
- 5-year certification, renewable with inspection
