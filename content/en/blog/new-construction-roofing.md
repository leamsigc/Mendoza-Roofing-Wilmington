## What We Install for New Construction

### Asphalt Shingles (Architectural & Impact-Resistant)

The most common choice for new residential construction in Wilmington. We install:
- **Architectural shingles** — dimensional look, 25–30 year lifespan in coastal NC
- **Impact-resistant shingles** — rated for hail and high-wind events, often eligible for insurance discounts in Brunswick County

We work with leading manufacturers including GAF, CertainTeed, and Owens Corning, and are certified applicators for manufacturer warranty programs.

### Standing Seam Metal Roofing

The **fastest-growing choice for new coastal construction** in Wilmington. Standing seam metal offers:
- 40–70 year expected lifespan in coastal NC conditions
- Superior wind resistance (rated for 140+ mph winds on most systems)
- Low maintenance compared to shingles
- Excellent salt-air corrosion resistance when properly specified

We install standing seam metal for everything from custom homes on Oak Island to multi-unit residential developments in Leland.

### Corrugated Metal Roofing

More affordable than standing seam with great durability. Common on agricultural, commercial, and workshop buildings throughout Brunswick County.

### Tile Roofing (Clay & Concrete)

For high-end residential builds in Wilmington and along the coast. Clay and concrete tile offer exceptional longevity and distinct coastal aesthetics, with proper underlayment critical for NC coastal climate.

### Flat Roof Systems (TPO, EPDM, Modified Bitumen)

For commercial buildings, multi-family structures, or flat/low-slope sections of a custom home. We install and certify flat roof systems for commercial projects across Wilmington.
