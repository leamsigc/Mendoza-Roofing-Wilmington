### The 15 Questions You Must Ask Before Hiring a Roofing Contractor

#### 1. **Are you licensed and insured in North Carolina?**
   - A reputable contractor will display a valid NC roofing license and carry general liability, workers' comp, and builder's risk insurance. Ask for proof.

#### 2. **How long have you been in business?**
   - Local expertise often comes from a history of working in the area. A contractor with 10+ years in the region knows common failure points and repair best practices.

#### 3. **Do you have North Carolina-specific references?**
   - Request at least three recent local references (preferably homeowners in Wilmington or Brunswick County). Follow up with the references yourself.

#### 4. **What roofing materials do you recommend for my roof type and climate?**
   - Coastal winds favor metal or impact-rated shingles; older asphalt roofs may need replacement rather than repair.

#### 5. **Will you remove the old roof before installing a new one?**
   - Removing the old layer ensures proper flashing, underlayment, and a clean surface for the new roof. Skipping this step can lead to premature failure.

#### 6. **What warranty do you offer on workmanship and materials?**
   - Look for a written warranty covering both the contractor's labor and the manufacturer's product. A 5-year workmanship warranty is a good benchmark.

#### 7. **Can you provide a detailed written estimate?**
   - A good estimate lists every material, the number of hours of labor, and the total price. Avoid contractors who give only a ballpark figure.

#### 8. **Do you use subcontractors, and are they licensed?**
   - If you hire a general contractor, ensure any subcontractors they bring in are vetted and covered by your main contract.

#### 9. **What's your process for handling insurance claims?**
   - A reliable contractor will help you file a claim, document damage, and coordinate with the adjuster without charging extra.

#### 10. **What time-of-day or season rates apply?**
   - Emergency repairs (e.g., after a storm) usually cost more than scheduled work. Knowing the rate helps budget.

#### 11. **How do you protect my property during installation?**
   - Look for tarps, walk-on pads, and careful handling of gutters, landscaping, and belongings.

#### 12. **Do you offer a post-project inspection?**
   - A final walk-through ensures the work meets standards and any issues are corrected.

#### 13. **Can I see a copy of your permits?**
   - Proper permits are required for roof replacements; the contractor should provide them.

#### 14. **Do you have a history of on-time completions?**
   - Ask about typical project timelines‚Äîmost residential roofs are finished in 1-3 days.

#### 15. **Are you certified for the roofing systems you install?**
   - Look for certifications from manufacturers such as GAF, CertainTeed, or certified ‚ÄúFortified‚Äù roofing programs.

### Common Roof Problems in Wilmington, Supply & Brunswick County

- **Wind damage**: Missing or curled shingles, lifted flashing.
- **Hail damage**: Denting metal roofs, cracking asphalt tiles.
- **Moisture & mold**: Leaks from faulty flashing or clogged gutters.
- **Salt corrosion**: Metal gutters, fascia, and flashings deteriorate faster along the coast.

Recognizing these issues early lets you negotiate a fair repair price and avoid costly replacements.
