# Gutter Installation & Repair in Wilmington, NC

A healthy gutter system is essential for protecting your Wilmington NC home from water damage. Poorly functioning gutters lead to foundation erosion, basement flooding, fascia rot, and landscape damage. **Mendoza Roofing LLC** provides professional gutter installation, repair, and replacement throughout Wilmington, Brunswick County, and all surrounding areas.

---

## Gutter Services We Offer in Wilmington NC

### Seamless Gutter Installation
We install seamless aluminum gutters custom-fabricated on-site to fit your home perfectly. Seamless gutters eliminate joints (a major source of leaks) and provide:
- Custom fit to every roof line
- No mid-run joints or seams
- Available in 5" K-Style and 6" K-Style sizes
- Wide range of colors to match your trim

### Gutter Repair
- Reattaching sagging or pulling-away gutters
- Sealing leaky joints and corners
- Replacing damaged sections
- Fixing improper slope causing standing water

### Downspout Installation & Repair
- Proper downspout placement and sizing
- Extensions and underground drainage connections
- Splash block installation

### Fascia & Soffit Repair
Damaged gutters often lead to rotted fascia and soffit. We repair and replace:
- Rotted or damaged fascia boards
- Soffit panels
- Drip edge installation for proper water diversion

### Gutter Guard Installation
Wilmington's abundant tree canopy means gutters fill with leaves and debris quickly. Gutter guards reduce maintenance and prevent clogs.

---

## Why Gutters Are Critical in Wilmington NC

Wilmington receives an average of **54 inches of rainfall per year** — 30% more than the US average. Without properly functioning gutters:
- Foundation erosion and settling
- Basement and crawlspace water intrusion
- Fascia and soffit rot leading to mold
- Siding staining and damage
- Landscape erosion and soil displacement
