## Why Get a Roof Inspection?

### After a Storm
Wilmington gets hit by tropical storms, hurricanes, and severe thunderstorms regularly. Even if you don't see damage, high winds can lift shingles, loosen flashing, and create vulnerabilities that lead to leaks months later.

### Before Buying or Selling
A professional roof inspection protects both buyers and sellers. Buyers know exactly what they're getting into. Sellers can address issues before listing and avoid surprises during negotiations.

### Routine Maintenance
Catching small issues early saves thousands. A missing shingle, cracked flashing boot, or minor leak caught today costs $150-400 to fix. The same issue left for 6 months can cause $5,000+ in interior water damage.
