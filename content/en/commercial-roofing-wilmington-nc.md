## Commercial Roofing Services We Offer in Wilmington

### Flat Roof Installation & Replacement
Most commercial buildings in Wilmington have flat or low-slope roofs. We install all major commercial roofing systems:

**TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin)**
- Highly reflective — reduces cooling costs in Wilmington's hot summers
- Excellent UV resistance and flexibility
- Resists punctures, tears, and chemical exposure
- 15-20+ year lifespan with proper maintenance

**EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer)**
- One of the most durable commercial flat roofing materials
- Excellent UV and temperature resistance
- Cost-effective long-term solution
- 20-30+ year lifespan

**Modified Bitumen**
- Multi-layer system for added protection
- Suitable for high-traffic commercial roofs
- Torch-applied or self-adhered options

**EDS Waterproof Membranes**
Mendoza Roofing is an **approved EDS installer** — a premium waterproofing system ideal for flat commercial roofs in coastal environments like Wilmington where water management is critical.
