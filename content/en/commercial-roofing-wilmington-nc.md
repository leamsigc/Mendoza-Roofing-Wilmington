## Commercial Roofing Systems We Install in Wilmington NC

Most commercial buildings in Wilmington have **flat or low-slope roofs**. We install and service a wide range of **commercial roofing systems**, combining the system menu and benefits you see on the top competitor pages - plus our EDS specialization.

### TPO Roofing (Thermoplastic Polyolefin)

- Highly reflective white surface that helps **cut cooling costs** in Wilmington's hot, sunny summers  
- Excellent **UV resistance** and flexibility  
- Resists **punctures, tears, and chemical exposure**  
- Typical lifespan: **15-20+ years** with proper maintenance  
- Ideal for warehouses, retail, and industrial buildings

### EPDM Roofing (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer)

- Time-tested **rubber roofing membrane** with outstanding durability  
- Handles extreme temperature shifts and UV exposure  
- Cost-effective for large flat roofs  
- Typical lifespan: **20-30+ years**  
- Common on office buildings, medical facilities, and multi-family properties

### PVC & Modified Bitumen

**PVC Roofing**

- Excellent **chemical and grease resistance**, ideal for restaurants and industrial settings  
- Heat-welded seams provide strong waterproofing  
- Can be specified as part of **cool roof** assemblies

**Modified Bitumen**

- Multi-layer system for extra protection  
- Great for **high-traffic roofs** with equipment and foot traffic  
- Available in torch-applied or self-adhered options

### Metal, SPF & EDS Waterproof Membranes

**Standing-Seam Metal Roofing**

- Long-lasting, highly wind-resistant, and ideal for coastal exposure  
- Often used on office buildings, churches, and retail centers  
- Can be combined with **reflective coatings** for energy efficiency

**SPF (Spray Polyurethane Foam) & Coatings**

- Excellent for restoring older flat roofs without full tear-off  
- Provides added insulation and seamless waterproofing  
- Silicone and reflective coatings **extend roof life** and improve energy performance

**EDS Waterproof Membranes**

Mendoza Roofing is an **approved EDS installer**. These membranes are engineered for **flat and low-slope roofs in coastal environments** like Wilmington, where humidity and heavy storms are constant challenges. EDS systems help keep water out and extend roof life significantly.

We designed our inspection process to be as thorough as or better than national providers:
